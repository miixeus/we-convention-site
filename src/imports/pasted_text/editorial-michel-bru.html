Create a one-page editorial style website inspired by classic American newspaper and magazine layouts. The site represents a couple from Brazil (Michel & Bru) and must feel timeless, elegant, minimal and personal. STYLE DIRECTION The design should look like a modern editorial page or a sophisticated printed journal. Avoid typical modern website UI such as large navigation menus or many buttons. There should be NO navigation menu. Sections should flow naturally through vertical storytelling. The visual impact must come mainly from typography, photography and white space. Use a calm, refined aesthetic similar to high-end editorial publications. TYPOGRAPHY (very important) Primary headline font: elegant serif similar to Playfair Display, Canela or Cormorant Garamond. Secondary text font: clean editorial sans-serif similar to Inter or Source Sans. Large typographic compositions are encouraged. Use oversized typography occasionally in the background for visual identity. Example: Large word in background: “BRAZIL” COLOR PALETTE Ivory background: #F8F6F2 Warm sand: #E8E2D6 Deep forest: #1F2A28 Charcoal: #2F2F2F Soft gold accents: #C6A15B The design must be mostly neutral with subtle warm tones. GRAPHIC ELEMENTS Use thin editorial divider lines. Use minimal geometric accents. Avoid emoji icons. Use subtle frames around photos. Spacing and white space are extremely important. SITE STRUCTURE SECTION 1 — HERO Large full width hero with editorial typography. Title: Michel & Bru Subtitle: From Cuiabá, Brazil Short text: “A quiet life, warm friendships and a love for simple things.” Large elegant photograph in the background or beside the text. Use refined typography hierarchy and generous white space. SECTION 2 — OUR LIFE Editorial layout with alternating large photographs and short text blocks. Each photograph includes a small caption below. Example captions: “Our home city, Cuiabá” “Sharing Bible hope in our territory” “Nature around our region” “Moments with dear friends” Layout style should resemble a magazine article layout. Images can be large, asymmetrical and elegant. SECTION 3 — FRIENDS AROUND THE WORLD This is the only interactive section of the website. Create a refined world map interface integrated into the editorial layout. Title: Friends Around the World Interaction behavior: Visitors can click on the map and place a pin indicating where they are from. When placing a pin they can upload: • their name • their country • a small photo • a short message Each pin placed on the map becomes an interactive message balloon. When the mouse hovers over the pin: a floating card appears showing • the visitor photo • their name • their country • their message Pins accumulate on the map forming a visual network of friends around the world. The visual style of the map should remain minimal and elegant. Pins should be subtle markers, not cartoon icons. SECTION 4 — MILWAUKEE MEMORY A short reflective editorial section. Title: Milwaukee 2026 Short paragraph expressing the joy of meeting brothers and sisters from many parts of the world. Include a few elegant photos with captions. SECTION 5 — CLOSING Minimal closing section. Text: “Thank you for visiting our page.” Below it: Michel & Bru Cuiabá — Brazil Use elegant typography and minimal design. DESIGN CHARACTERISTICS The page must feel calm, editorial and timeless. Use large typography moments. Use thin dividers. Use elegant photography. Avoid excessive UI elements. Focus on storytelling and atmosphere.
